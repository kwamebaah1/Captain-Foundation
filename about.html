ABOUT US

The Captain One Foundation

Captain One Gold Golf Society (C1GS) is a society made up of golfers and non-golfers with the sole aim of developing the game of golf at the grassroots level.
The society also has no political affiliations whatsoever. It was founded by former Captain of Damang Golf Club, Mr Pius Ayeh Appiah who is affectionately called ‘Captain 1’ by his peers. The society’s vision and mission statement coupled with our statement of core values informs the procedures and quality of our decision making to achieve excellent results in the game of Golf.
